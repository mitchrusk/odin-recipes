The Grilled Cheese Sandwich

The Grilled Cheese Sandwhich is an American classic.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • Butter
  • American cheese slices

Procedure

  1. Place the cheese slices between the two slices of white bread.
  2. Butter both outer sides of the bread.
  3. Place on a hot pan until the bread becomes a toasted-golden brown.
  4. Flip the sandwhich and toast the other side.
  5. Remove from the heat and enjoy.
