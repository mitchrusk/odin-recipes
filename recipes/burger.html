Burger Recipe

Description

Indulge in the savory simplicity of this homemade burger recipe, a culinary delight that promises a symphony of flavors and textures. Crafted from 80% lean ground beef, seasoned with a pinch of salt and pepper, and grilled to perfection, the juicy and tender patties serve as the heart of this classic dish. Nestled within lightly toasted buns, these beefy creations are adorned with an array of toppings, including crisp lettuce, ripe tomatoes, onions, and your choice of cheese, pickles, ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise. The step-by-step process ensures a hassle-free preparation: from preheating the grill and shaping the patties to the final assembly, each stage contributes to the irresistible appeal of these homemade burgers. Whether enjoyed on its own or paired with favorite side dishes, this recipe invites a taste of comfort and satisfaction in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Toppings: lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, pickles, ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, etc.

Steps

  1. Preheat grill or pan.
  2. Shape beef into 4 patties; season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook patties for 4-5 mins per side (add cheese in the last minute).
  4. Cut and toast buns.
  5. Place patties on bottom bun; add toppings.
  6. Complete with top bun.
  7. Serve and enjoy with your favorite sides!
