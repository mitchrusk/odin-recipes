Chili Recipe

Description

Savor the simplicity of this easy homemade chili, a comforting dish that effortlessly combines hearty flavors and textures. In a large pot, brown 1 pound of ground beef, then sauté diced onions and minced garlic until softened. To this rich base, add a can of diced tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, and sweet corn, along with a can of tomato paste. Enhance the mixture with a blend of chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper, and pour in 2 cups of beef or vegetable broth. Simmer the chili to perfection, allowing the ingredients to meld for at least 20-30 minutes. The result is a robust and flavorful chili that can be customized with optional toppings such as shredded cheese, sour cream, and green onions. Serve hot and relish in the warmth and deliciousness of this quick and satisfying homemade creation.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned)
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 2 cups beef or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, green onions

Steps

  1. In a large pot, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add diced onions and minced garlic to the pot; sauté until onions are softened.
  3. Stir in diced tomatoes, kidney beans, black beans, corn, and tomato paste.
  4. Pour in the broth and add chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Bring the chili to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and let it cook for at least 20-30 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
  6. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  7. Serve hot, topped with shredded cheese, sour cream, and chopped green onions if desired. Enjoy your quick and delicious homemade chili!
